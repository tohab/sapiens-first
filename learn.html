The
Handbook

Our strategy, demands, and principles for building a movement that puts transformative artificial intelligence in the hands of the people.

Overview

Executive Summary

  • We are amidst an AI crisis that threatens our way of life.
  • We demand all world governments Declare the AI crisis, Democratize power over AI through assemblies, and Govern frontier systems like other existential threats.
  • We pursue exponential growth using peer-to-peer recruitment and distributed leadership — anyone following our values can organize under the brand.
  • Our tactical cycle: Connect (door-knocking, deep listening) → Learn (conference calls, small-group discussion) → Act (confronting politicians, disruptions, canvassing) → Party (celebration and community-building).
  • We call on politicians, corporations, and civil society to take a public stand on the crisis.
Why We Exist

Mission

To put transformative artificial intelligence in the hands of the people.

What We're Up Against

Crisis

Powerful AI could be here this decade. Such a force could destroy our economic system, or democracy, or even kill us all. Our politicians are sleeping at the wheel.

What We're Asking For

Demands

Sapiens First demands that all world governments:

01
Declare
Declare the AI crisis as a threat to our lives and livelihood. Experts have said for years that superhuman intelligence will take our jobs, destroy our democracy, or kill us all. We are at the point where this threat cannot be ignored.
02
Democratize
Democratize power over intelligence through citizen assemblies. Intelligence can be used to uplift humanity — but only if it is in the hands of the people. The power of AI should be directed by the public, not large companies or corrupt politicians. Citizens should decide how we navigate the ongoing economic transition.
03
Govern
Govern superintelligence like we govern other existential threats. Similar to nuclear weapons, we must have peace treaties and arms control agreements with all other countries, independent safety reviews, and strict liability on frontier systems.
Our Strategy

Movement Principles

To win, Sapiens First must mobilize millions to transform our political system before the creation of superintelligence. Exponential growth is the path to victory — and this is done with distributed leadership, scale, and confrontation.

Our strategy is drawn from a lineage of highly successful, viral social movements including the Tea Party, Extinction Rebellion, and Otpor.

Empowerment
Speaking truth to power is empowering. It also builds leaders who organize and recruit others to join the fight.
Distribution
It is easy to start a group in one's own city. Our brand is reusable, and our tactics work anywhere.
Direct Action
By confronting the holders of power — our politicians — we disrupt "business as usual" and challenge them to take a stand.
Taking Action

Politicians Have the Power

Our politicians can stop the AI crisis if they choose to act. We confront politicians in public and demand they take a stand on the issue. If they don't, we continue to confront, disrupt, and recruit to raise the political cost of inaction.

Our actions can be done in any city around the US and other countries. To create an action, you just have to choose where, when, and how.

Where
  • Civic centers — city halls, state capitols, congressional offices
  • Public spaces — town squares, university campuses, farmer's markets
When
  • Recurring government meetings (city councils, county boards, state legislature hearings)
  • Public events (town halls, office hours, candidate forums)
How
  • An action is a story. Make it funny, engaging, and sympathetic.
  • A clear ask — Declare, Democratize, and/or Govern.

The Playbook

How to Win

Step 01
Connect
  • Knock on the door of a stranger in your neighborhood. By connecting with a stranger, you are expanding your network and tapping into theirs.
  • Listen most of the time (80%). When people are listened to, they feel heard and cared for. This is empowering.
  • Invite the person to share more at an upcoming online meeting.
Step 02
Learn
  • Present the broad goals of Sapiens First and what we're up against.
  • Share your concerns about AI in groups of 3–6 people. Social science tells us that small groups best distribute speaking time and thus empower participants.
  • Choose a pathway of action to explore in a subsequent call.
Step 03
Act

Activists join a Circle — based on their location and chosen Pathway — and choose exactly what to do. There are many possible options!

  • Confront politicians with speak-outs, mock funerals, public theater, group lobbying
  • Disrupt business-as-usual with die-ins, picket lines, sit-ins, walk-outs, seeking arrest
  • Recruit people via door-knocking, canvassing, tabling, public gatherings
Step 04
Party
  • Celebrate recent activities
  • Build trust by connecting with comrades in the fight in a less formal context
  • Ideate next steps
The Movement Structure

Onion Model

To be pluralistic, there should be something for everyone in the movement — like layers of an onion.

Participants
