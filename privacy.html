Policy

We collect your email address when you sign up. We use it to keep you informed about Sapiens First — actions, calls, and updates. We don't sell it, share it with third parties for marketing, or do anything sketchy with it. You can opt out any time.

What we collect

When you submit the signup form on our website, we collect your email address and the date and time of your submission. We do not collect your name, location, phone number, or any other personal information.

How we use it

We use your email address solely to send you updates about Sapiens First actions and events, invite you to movement calls and organizing opportunities, and connect you with others in your area. We process your data on the basis of legitimate interest in communicating with people who have chosen to join our movement.

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. Your data is stored securely.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain an active subscriber. If you unsubscribe or request deletion, we remove your data within 7 days.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time by emailing rohan@sapiensfirst.org. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link, or you can opt out by replying "unsubscribe" to any message. EU residents may also lodge a complaint with their national data protection authority.

Cookies & tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics software on this website.

Contact

Questions? Email us at rohan@sapiensfirst.org.
