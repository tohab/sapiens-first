
**Vision**
We imagine a world where technology serves the common good.

**Mission**
Our mission is to protect liberal democracy from the dangers of artificial intelligence.

**Theory of Change**
While artificial intelligence is being developed at a break-neck pace, our political system is not keeping up. We believe that a mass movement is necessary to create the changes necessary to save humanity in time for superintelligence. We do this by:
- Building a network of activists. We organize community gatherings, trainings, and workshops to build a base of committed, pro-democracy advocates.
- Challenging politicians. We run campaigns against politicians across the political spectrum to force them to take a stand on AI governance. We use strategic, sacrificial, and humorous non-violent direct action to build momentum and win public support.
- Local to nation. We start local, building communities in our city councils, churches, and universities, working up to state and national policy.

Our specific approach relies on a fly-wheel effect of civic engagement, drawn from past social movements:
- Act 
- Recruit 
- Train

**Focus Areas**
Policy recommendations: forthcoming
- **Democratic Renewal.** We believe that AI poses a dire threat to the foundations of American democracy, but also has the potential to greatly enhance it. AI enhances the functioning of the surveillance apparatus; wealth gains from AI have corrupted our electoral process; and unchecked, algorithimic-driven social media has created a severely polarized society. Democratic renewal means putting an end to these evils, and instead directing technology to enhance our democratic process.
- **Common Prosperity.** The gains of AI should be shared broadly, and intelligence should be directed to address societal problems. As with the Industrial Revolution, the gains from AI will increase the size of the pie and create vast abundant resources in the economy. However, currently, this wealth is likely to concentrate in the hands of the rich few, leading to the economic disenfranchisement of the majority of human beings. We believe that AI is a public good, and windfall profits from AI should be distributed through taxes, subsidies, and re-investing in public institutions. We also believe that publicly-directed AI should be used to optimize existing government institutions, and dramatically accelerate advances in medicine, science, and technology.
- **Protecting Humanity.** Much like nuclear weapons, powerful AI has the potential to cause mass devastation. We believe that major powers should lead a global treaty on the development of safe superintelligence. Frontier AI should be developed transparently, with extensive safety testing. Additionally, the creation of superintelligence should be a global project, with appropriate checks and balances so its power does not fall into one person's hands.
