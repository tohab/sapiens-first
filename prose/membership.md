## Our Core Values

Activists with Sapiens First must follow our Core Values:

**Peace.** We don’t threaten or harm people or property.

**Respect.** We are kind, listen, and use non-violent communication.

**Sustainability.** We support each other and take breaks.

**Intentionality.** We work smart, not hard, to make change.

## Membership agreement

Sapiens First may deny, suspend, or revoke membership or access to member spaces for serious or repeated violations of our Core Values, Community Guidelines, safety rules, or applicable law.

Except when immediate action is needed to protect someone’s safety, we will provide notice of the concern and a reasonable opportunity for the member to respond.

Membership does not authorize a person to speak, fundraise, organize activities, sign agreements, or otherwise act on behalf of Sapiens First unless explicitly authorized.
