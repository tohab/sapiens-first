Our Strategy

Theory of Change

While artificial intelligence is being developed at a break-neck pace, our political system is not keeping up. We believe that a mass movement is necessary to create the changes necessary to save humanity in time for superintelligence. We do this by:

Building a Network
We organize community gatherings, trainings, and workshops to build a base of committed, pro-democracy advocates.
Challenging Politicians
We run campaigns against politicians across the political spectrum to force them to take a stand on AI governance. We use strategic, sacrificial, and humorous non-violent direct action to build momentum and win public support.
Local to Nation
We start local, building communities in our city councils, churches, and universities, working up to state and national policy.
