AI is transforming everything. Who decides the rules?

The Guardrail Project is a civic movement building political power so that artificial intelligence serves the public interest — not just the companies that build it.

What We Fight For

Freedom

Privacy. Protection for children. The right to speak the truth about dangerous AI.

Democracy

Oversight of powerful AI. Distributed power. Elections free from AI manipulation.

Justice

Legal accountability. Creator rights. Shared prosperity from AI's gains.

We build political power from the ground up — organizing in cities and states to pass real legislation, just like the movements that won civil rights, marriage equality, and safe driving laws.
