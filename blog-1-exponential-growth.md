Here's 3 different models for having an impact.

### 1:1 Growth

Imagine y is the impact you create, and x is the amount of staff time you have. 

y = x

We call this 1:1 growth. x goes up, and our impact, y, goes up as well.

### Linear Growth

Imagine that each staff person coordinates A volunteers. 

Then, we have:

y = Ax

Linear growth. 

In political organizing, this is called the "snowflake model". It was the innovation of Obama's campaign in 2008.


### Exponential Growth

Imagine instead that every volunteer brings in two more volunteers. Then, you have exponential growth:

y = A^x
