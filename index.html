The Guardrail Project

Turning public concern into tech accountability.

The Guardrail Project connects voters and civic organizations across the country, channeling that energy into sustained pressure on local representatives — through letter-writing campaigns, petitions, townhalls, and demonstrations. State by state, we build the political will for serious tech regulation, and the traction to take it federal. Americans across the political spectrum agree: AI needs rules, and the companies won't write them.
