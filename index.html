THEORY OF CHANGE

Real change starts local.

01
Local Resolutions
We pass resolutions in city councils calling for AI accountability. Each resolution builds our ability to organize and demonstrates public will for meaningful legislation.
02
State Laws
State campaigns turn our proposals into actual policy. We take the resolutions, coalitions, and momentum built by local campaigns to statehouses in red and blue states alike.
03
Federal Policy
With active chapters and campaigns in every major district, we make the issue of AI impossible to ignore, and give lawmakers the push they need to act.
POLICY

Our reforms would change the balance of power in this era.

01
AI Dividend
The boons of AI should be shared with all American people. We advocate for a public dividend that gives Americans a stake in the gains of technology, if AI meaningfully displaces workers.45
02
Legal Liability
Bad actors and negligent developers should be held accountable for their actions. We demand strict liability on frontier systems, and compensated damages brought by systems to individuals and society.6
03
Democratic Oversight
We can't let the power of AI be concentrated in the hands of corporate interests or the government. We advocate for an independent agency that audits labs and makes treaties with foreign powers, with input from civil society.78
