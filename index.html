PLATFORM

Our reforms would shift the balance of power.

01
Common Prosperity
If AI displaces workers at scale, the gains belong to all Americans — not just shareholders. We advocate for a public dividend tied to AI-driven productivity growth.45
02
Democratic Oversight
No corporation and no government should control AI alone. We advocate for an independent oversight agency with audit powers, treaty authority, and mandatory civil society input.78
03
Safety & Accountability
Right now, AI developers bear almost no legal responsibility for the harm their systems cause. We demand strict liability for frontier AI — the same standard we hold every other powerful industry to.6
