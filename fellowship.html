Sapiens First is building a movement to put powerful AI in the hands of the people. We imagine a world where AI serves the common good. We fight for democracy, prosperity, and security in the age of AI. You can learn more about our theory of change on our About page.

The Role

We're founding a volunteer team to build the movement's early infrastructure. We currently have three roles available.

Community Fellow
In-person · SF Bay

You enjoy talking to new people, are a great listener, and care deeply about community. You're great at making people feel empowered, with the goal of them joining the movement.

Example activities
  • Designing events for people
  • Door-to-door conversations with people
  • Creating curriculum and documentation for volunteers to train up

Community Fellows should be able to commute to SF, Berkeley, or Oakland about twice a week to help with in-person community building.

Systems Fellow
Remote-friendly

You enjoy building scalable systems and automations to empower communities. You're a fast learner and excellent at incorporating AI to assist you in the process, with the goal of streamlining our supporter and membership onboarding and retention processes.

Example activities
  • Rigging up an open-source CRM to our membership sign-up page
  • Creating an email automation protocol for sign-ups, event attendees, and supporters
  • Automating member onboarding on Discord
Marketing Fellow
Remote-friendly

You enjoy communicating big ideas clearly and learning what inspires people to take action. You will test messaging and branding, with the goal of expanding our digital reach.

Example activities
  • Refining our brand marketing strategy
  • Designing and executing our digital marketing strategy
  • Designing materials like business cards, banners, and lawn signs
Who We're Looking For

You may be a strong fit if you:

  • Care deeply about how advanced AI will affect humanity
  • Want to help build an ambitious movement from an early stage
  • Take initiative and follow through reliably
  • Are comfortable experimenting and learning quickly
  • Welcome direct feedback
  • Care about measurable results

Formal credentials are not required. We care more about judgment, initiative, reliability, and demonstrated ability to get things done.

What Fellows Receive

This is an unpaid volunteer fellowship. Fellows receive meaningful ownership, close collaboration with the founder, regular feedback and coaching, reimbursement for approved work expenses, and meals during relevant in-person activities. Strong fellows may be invited to extend their work after the initial term.

Commitment

The fellowship lasts 12 weeks and requires approximately 8–12 hours per week. Scheduling is generally flexible, with a regular weekly meeting.

How to Apply

We're excited to hear from you! Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. Please email questions to rohan@sapiensfirst.org.
