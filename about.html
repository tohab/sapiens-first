We make change by building community and peacefully disrupting the status quo.

We invite people to connect through one-on-one conversations.
Disrupt
Our disruptions are good-natured but direct, challenging politicians to act.
Core Values

Activists with Sapiens First must follow our Core Values.

  • Peace. We don't threaten or harm people or property.
  • Respect. We are kind, listen, and use non-violent communication.
  • Sustainability. We support each other and take breaks.
  • Intentionality. We work smart, not hard, to make change.
