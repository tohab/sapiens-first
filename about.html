Our Vision

We imagine a world where AI serves the common good. Our campaigns focus on:

We believe that AI poses a dire threat to the foundations of American democracy, but also has the potential to greatly enhance it. AI strengthens the surveillance apparatus; wealth gains from AI have corrupted our electoral process; and unchecked, algorithmic social media has created a severely polarized society. Democratic renewal means putting an end to these evils, and instead directing technology to enhance our democratic process.

The gains of AI should be shared broadly, and intelligence should be directed to address societal problems. As with the Industrial Revolution, the gains from AI will increase the size of the pie and create vast abundant resources in the economy. However, currently, this wealth is likely to concentrate in the hands of the rich few, leading to the economic disenfranchisement of the majority of human beings. We believe that AI is a public good, and windfall profits from AI should be distributed through taxes, subsidies, and re-investing in public institutions. We also believe that publicly-directed AI should be used to optimize existing government institutions, and dramatically accelerate advances in medicine, science, and technology.

Much like nuclear weapons, powerful AI has the potential to cause mass devastation. We believe that major powers should lead a global treaty on the development of safe superintelligence. Frontier AI should be developed transparently, with extensive safety testing. Additionally, the creation of superintelligence should be a global project, with appropriate checks and balances so its power does not fall into one person's hands.

Core Values

Activists with Sapiens First must follow our Core Values.

  • Peace. We don't threaten or harm people or property.
  • Respect. We are kind, listen, and use non-violent communication.
  • Sustainability. We support each other and take breaks.
  • Intentionality. We work smart, not hard, to make change.

Sapiens First is a non-profit organization founded by Rohan Prasad. We are supported by private donors, foundations, and our community members.
